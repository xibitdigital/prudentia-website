About me

Dr. Sonia Sangalli is a Chartered Counselling Psychologist trained and based in London, UK with over 19 years' clinical experience including delivering workshops for corporate organisations.

How I work

My approach is thoughtful, practical, and focused. We won't just talk in circles—we'll work to understand what's going on beneath the surface and identify what needs to change.

I help people:

  • Make sense of what they're feeling
  • Break unhelpful patterns
  • Improve relationships
  • Regain clarity, direction and a sense of control

Many people I work with are used to handling things on their own. Therapy offers a space where you can step out of that, make sense of what's going on, and not have to carry everything alone.
