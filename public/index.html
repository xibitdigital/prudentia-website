My Therapeutic Approach

A collaborative, evidence-based approach that combines the proven effectiveness of CBT with personalised care tailored to your unique situation.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

CBT is a structured therapy that focuses on identifying and changing your relationship with your thought patterns, emotions and behaviours. It's one of the most researched and effective forms of psychotherapy.

1

Assessment & Goal Setting

We begin by understanding your specific challenges and collaboratively setting clear, achievable goals for therapy.

2

Skill Development

Develop greater understanding of yourself and what impacts your approach to life through CBT formulation techniques and learn practical ways for managing thoughts, emotions, and behaviours through structured exercises and homework assignments.

3

Practice & Integration

Apply new skills in real-life situations with ongoing support and adjustment of strategies as needed.
